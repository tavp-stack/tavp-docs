## Usage Guidelines

### Do

- Use logo on dark backgrounds
- Maintain minimum clear space (size of letter "T")
- Use official logos from this page

### Don't

- Change logo colors
- Rotate or stretch logo
- Add shadow or glow effects
- Use logo to mislead
